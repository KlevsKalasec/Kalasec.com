Common questions

Is Kalasec currently a registered OJK assessor?
No. Kalasec provides technical security assessments. Official OJK compliance submissions require a vendor on OJK's registered list. We can connect you with registered partners, and we frequently run technical work in parallel with their official filing process.
Can I white-label your work without disclosing Kalasec?
Yes. White-label engagements are fully confidential. We sign an NDA before any work begins and operate entirely under your brand. Deliverables carry your name. Kalasec is not mentioned in any client-facing document.
How is the referral fee calculated and paid?
10–15% of the closed engagement fee, paid within 30 days of delivery confirmation. The exact rate depends on engagement size and volume. We agree on the rate in writing before you make any referral.
Can we partner for clients outside Indonesia?
Yes. We work with clients globally. Our regional expertise covers Southeast Asia, but technical security assessments apply to any organisation anywhere. If your client is in Europe, the US, or the Middle East, we can still execute.
What if a client already has an OJK assessor?
That's a common scenario. We run the technical assessment in parallel with the official assessor's process. The registered assessor handles the compliance filing. We handle the technical execution — typically faster and at higher coverage. Both deliverables go to the client.

Fill in the inquiry form above or email partner@kalasec.com. We'll respond within 1 business day to schedule a brief call and discuss fit.
